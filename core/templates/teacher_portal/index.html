1. Build Your Advisory Curriculum

Use SIS Import or manual creation to add Subjects. Then create Class Offerings for your section (e.g., Math 10 - Rizal) and assign yourself as the teacher.

2. Enroll Students

Add New Students (accounts) to your roster. To save time, use Quick Enroll to Advisory Group above to instantly assign students to all your class offerings at once!
